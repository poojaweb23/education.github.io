About Us

Welcome to MOVE Law Academy, your premier destination for a transformative legal education experience. Our dedicated team, comprising former judges, high court advocates, renowned academicians and esteemed alumni from National Law Universities (NLUs), stands ready to guide you on your journey to legal excellence.

At MOVE, we pride ourselves on pioneering innovative teaching methodologies that go beyond the conventional. Our courses, ranging from Judicial Examinations, CLAT UG and PG coaching to specialized UGC NET Law preparation, offer a comprehensive suite of legal education options. We believe in making the learning process engaging and effective, utilizing 2D animated movies, motion graphics, flowcharts, and mnemonic tricks to simplify complex legal concepts. As your one-step solution for all your law-related needs, MOVE Law Academy also provides specialized coaching for the All India Bar Examination (AIBE) and offers certified courses in various legal domains. Additionally, our test series, meticulously crafted for Judiciary Exams, CLAT (UG and PG), All India Bar Examination and UGC NET, provide a realistic exam simulation, ensuring you are well-prepared and confident on the day of your examination.

Join us at MOVE Law Academy, where every class is a step toward legal mastery, and where our team is dedicated to empowering your success in the legal arena. Your MOVE toward a rewarding legal career begins here.
